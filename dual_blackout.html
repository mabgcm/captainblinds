Dual Blackout Shades – Sunshade & Curtain in One

Dual Blackout Shades (also known as Double Blackout Shades or Double Roller Shades) act as both a sunshade and a curtain in a single compact system. The mechanism uses two separate rollers: one carries a blackout roller shade, while the other carries a light-filtering or sheer tulle layer.

Each layer is controlled independently, giving you the freedom to soften daylight with the sheer layer, create full privacy and darkness with the blackout layer, or combine both depending on the time of day. This flexibility makes Dual Blackout Shades a stylish and practical solution for living rooms, bedrooms, offices and any space with strong sunlight.

Space-Saving, Stylish Window Coverings

Unlike traditional bulky curtains, Dual Blackout Shades sit neatly at the top of the window and do not take up extra floor or wall space. Because the two fabrics are mounted on a double-roller system, they move smoothly and stack compactly when raised, keeping your windows looking clean and uncluttered.

The blackout and sheer layers are carefully selected to complement each other, so you can achieve a coordinated colour scheme and a refined, contemporary look. If you prefer a unique combination, you can choose custom fabric and colour options to suit your interior design.

Quality Materials & Custom Manufacturing

Our Dual Blackout Shades are produced using the same level of quality and attention to detail as our most popular blind collections. Fabrics are chosen for durability, colour stability and ease of cleaning, and every shade is custom-made to your window measurements for a precise fit.

  • Two-in-one system: blackout roller shade plus sheer or tulle layer
  • Individually controlled layers for flexible light and privacy
  • Compact design that saves space compared to traditional curtains
  • Coordinated colours for a modern, elegant appearance
  • Custom-made to your exact window sizes

UV Protection & Comfort

When the blackout layer is fully closed, Dual Blackout Shades help protect your floors, furniture and décor from the harmful effects of prolonged sun exposure by significantly reducing UV rays coming into the room. This makes them an ideal solution in spaces where you want to enjoy natural light at times, but also need full darkness for sleeping, relaxing or watching TV.

We are confident that you will be highly satisfied with the comfort, control and style that Dual Blackout Shades bring to your home or workplace.

Product Features & Lift Options

  • Double-roller system with separate blackout and sheer layers
  • Each layer operated independently for precise light control
  • Lift systems available with chain guard, wand motorized and remote control
  • Optional smartphone and Alexa-compatible smart home control
  • Suitable for bedrooms, living rooms, offices and large window areas
  • Engineered for smooth operation and long-term daily use

How to Order Dual Blackout Shades

Ordering Dual Blackout Shades with CaptainBlinds is simple. Start by contacting us for a free, no-obligation consultation. Tell us about your windows, the rooms they are in and how you would like to control light and privacy. We will then arrange an in-home visit to show you fabric and colour options for both the blackout and sheer layers.

  • Review blackout and sheer fabric options
  • Select colours that coordinate with your décor
  • Choose your preferred lift and control system
  • Have each window measured for exact sizing
  • Approve your final configuration before production

Delivery & Fitting Guide

Once your fabrics and lift system are confirmed, your Dual Blackout Shades are manufactured to your exact measurements and prepared for installation. Our professional installers then return to fit the system, test the operation of both layers and make any final adjustments so the shades run smoothly and align perfectly.

For more information on production times, delivery and installation, or to schedule your free consultation, call us at (416)985-3839 or contact us through our online form.
