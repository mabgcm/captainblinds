Blackout Roller Shades for Complete Light Control

Blackout Roller Shades are one of the most popular modern window treatments for people who want to darken a room, improve privacy and create a comfortable interior. Instead of traditional horizontal blinds with individual slats, blackout roller shades use a single piece of fabric that rolls up and down smoothly, giving your windows a sleek and tidy appearance.

They are a practical and attractive choice for covering standard windows as well as sliding doors. When lowered, blackout roller shades help reduce outside light, glare and heat, making them ideal for bedrooms, nurseries, media rooms and any space where you want a darker, more restful environment. At the same time, their clean lines and fabric options enhance the overall look of your décor.

High-Quality Fabrics & Custom Sizing

Our Blackout Roller Shades are made using high-quality polyester and metallic-based fabrics designed for long-term performance. Each shade is custom-made to the exact dimensions of your windows. We measure your windows, then manufacture the shades based on those measurements so they fit neatly and operate smoothly.

During your order process, you can choose the fabric, colour and style that best match your interior. From soft neutrals to darker tones, you can create a look that coordinates with your furniture and walls while achieving the level of light control you need.

How to Order Blackout Roller Shades

Ordering Blackout Roller Shades from CaptainBlinds is straightforward. Start by contacting us for a free consultation or quote. Tell us about your windows, the rooms you want to darken and your preferred look. We will guide you through fabric and colour choices, and help you decide on the best lift system for your home.

  • Discuss your room, light and privacy needs
  • Choose blackout fabrics and colours that suit your décor
  • Select your preferred lift system and accessories
  • Let us measure each window precisely
  • We custom-manufacture your blackout shades
  • Our team completes a professional installation

Delivery & Fitting Guide

Once fabric and options are confirmed, your Blackout Roller Shades are produced to size and prepared for installation. When your order is ready, our installers visit your home to fit the shades, check that they roll smoothly and ensure that the brackets and hardware are secure.
