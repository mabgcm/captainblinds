Galaxy Curtains & Drapes (Vertical Zebra Curtains & Drapes)

Galaxy Curtains & Drapes, also known as Vertical Zebra Curtains & Drapes, are a frequently preferred model in both homes and workplaces. With their vertical fabric bands and clean lines, they fit beautifully into modern interiors and help create a stylish, contemporary look.

These curtains are especially suitable for large windows and patio doors because they can be neatly collected to one side, both sides, or opened from the middle. The bands are carried on wagons that move along an aluminium rail, allowing you to draw the curtain to the right or left as needed. Using a simple chain control, the bands rotate around their own axis so you can adjust the angle of the light precisely.

Control Sunlight, Heat & Privacy

Vertical Zebra Curtains & Drapes make it possible to benefit from daylight at the level and angle you want. By rotating the bands, you can reduce glare on screens, soften harsh sunlight or close the curtains for privacy and dimming. This flexibility allows you to control not only the brightness but also the heat entering the room, making your space more comfortable throughout the day.

Durable & Easy to Maintain

Galaxy Curtains & Drapes are designed to be long-lasting and resistant to everyday impacts and use. The mechanism allows the system to be opened, collected and closed smoothly, while the cord or chain enables you to set the sun’s angle exactly as you prefer. The fabrics are chosen for their resistance to ageing, and cleaning is practical and straightforward, which makes these curtains a smart choice for busy homes and workplaces.

Custom-Made to Fit Your Windows

Our Vertical Zebra Curtains & Drapes are produced using high-quality polyester and metallic yarns. Each set is fully customized to the dimensions of your windows: we measure your windows, manufacture the curtains according to those measurements, and then professionally install them for a perfect, level finish. When placing your order, you can choose the colour, fabric and configuration that best suits your décor and functional needs.

UV Protection for Your Interior

Galaxy Curtains & Drapes help protect your floors, furniture and décor from the damage caused by prolonged sun exposure. When the bands are fully closed, they can block up to 99.9% of harmful UV rays, helping preserve the look and life of your interior while still giving you control over privacy and comfort.

Product Features & Installation

  • Ideal for large windows, patio doors and wide glass surfaces
  • Vertical bands carried on durable aluminium rails
  • Bands can be rotated to adjust light, glare and privacy
  • System can be drawn to the right, left or from the middle to both sides
  • Operated with a simple chain or cord control, with optional motorized systems
  • Resistant to daily use and designed for long-lasting performance
  • Easy to clean and maintain compared to many traditional curtain styles
  • Measured and installed by our professional team in Toronto & the GTA

How to Order Galaxy Curtains & Drapes

Ordering Galaxy Curtains & Drapes from CaptainBlinds is very simple. Call us, send a message or fill out the quote form and tell us about your windows and rooms. We will arrange a free, no-obligation consultation to visit your home or workplace, show you fabric samples, discuss colours and openness levels, and take precise measurements before your curtains are manufactured.

  • Share your window sizes, room type and light needs
  • Choose fabric, colour and band style that match your décor
  • Decide how the curtains should stack and which way they should open
  • We measure each window accurately on-site
  • Galaxy Curtains & Drapes are custom-made to your exact dimensions
  • Professional installation ensures a clean, level and smooth-running finish

Delivery & Fitting Guide

After your fabric and configuration choices are confirmed, your Galaxy Curtains & Drapes are produced to size and prepared for installation. Once they are ready, our team returns to fit the rails and bands, check the movement and light adjustment, and make any final tweaks so everything operates smoothly and stacks neatly.
