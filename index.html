How It Works

Our Simple 3-Step Working Process

From first visit to final installation, we keep the process clear, quick and hassle-free.

Step 01

Free In-Home Consultation

We visit your home, bring product samples, discuss your style and budget, and precisely measure each window.

Step 02

Custom Production

Once you approve the quote, your blinds, shades or curtains are custom-made to size using quality, durable materials.

Step 03

Professional Installation

Our installers fit and test every blind, clean up after the job and make sure you’re completely happy before we leave.
