Roman Shades – Soft Folds & Tailored Style

Roman shades combine the softness of fabric with the simple operation of a shade. When lowered, the fabric hangs flat or in gentle folds; when raised, it stacks into neat horizontal pleats, giving your windows a clean, tailored look without the bulk of traditional drapery.

Because Roman shades sit close to the window, they are ideal for spaces where you want a sleek, compact solution that still adds warmth, colour and texture. They work beautifully in living rooms, dining rooms, bedrooms and home offices, and can be made subtle and minimal or bold and decorative depending on the fabric you choose.

How to Order Roman Shades

Ordering Roman shades with CaptainBlinds is simple. Start by contacting us by phone, message or through our quote form. Tell us about your rooms, your light needs and any design ideas you have. We will then schedule a free in-home consultation to show you fabric samples, discuss fold styles and lining options, and take precise measurements for each window.

  • Choose fabric type, colour and texture to match your décor.
  • Select a fold style (flat, hobbled or relaxed) for the look you want.
  • Decide on light control: light-filtering, room-darkening or blackout lining.
  • Pick your preferred lift system: corded, cordless, continuous loop or motorized.
  • Have each shade custom-made to your exact window measurements.
  • Enjoy professional installation and a tidy, finished result in every room.

Delivery & Fitting Guide

Once your Roman shades are ordered, they are manufactured to size and carefully finished so the folds stack evenly and the fabric hangs smoothly. When your order is ready, our installation team returns to your home to fit each shade, test the operation and make any final adjustments.

For information on current lead times, measuring and installation, or to book your free in-home consultation in Toronto & the GTA, call us at (416)985-3839 or reach out via our contact form.
