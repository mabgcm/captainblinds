Smart, Motorized Shades

Simply modern and automated for your lifestyle – it’s your home and your light to control. With CaptainBlinds smart, motorized shades you can adjust privacy, light and comfort at the touch of a button or by using your voice.

Motorization & smart home solutions give you effortless control over your window coverings. Whether you want a single blind to move on command or an entire room to respond to a schedule, our automated options make everyday living more comfortable and convenient.

Motorization & Smart Home Solutions

Our powered blinds can be operated in many different ways: from simple handheld remotes and wall switches to home hubs that let you manage everything from your smartphone or tablet. Raise, lower or tilt your blinds from the sofa, your office or even while you’re away.

For added security and energy efficiency, you can program your blinds to open and close when you are not at home, at bedtime, in the evening or during key daylight hours. This helps keep rooms cooler in summer, warmer in winter and gives your home a lived-in look even when you are away.

  • Control blinds by hand, remote, app or voice
  • Create schedules for morning, evening and bedtime
  • Improve comfort, privacy and security automatically
  • Perfect for hard-to-reach or large windows

Smart Home Integrations

We use high-quality motors to operate the blinds and can integrate them with leading smart home systems so you can control everything with your preferred devices. Use your phone, tablet or voice assistants such as Amazon Alexa, Apple HomeKit and Google Assistant to move your blinds exactly how you want.

  • Operate blinds from remote controls or wall switches
  • Connect to home hubs and mobile apps for full control
  • Compatible with Alexa, Apple HomeKit and Google Assistant
  • Integrate with existing smart home solutions and scenes
  • Less manual use helps maintain a neat, “like new” appearance

Motorisation Options

Every home and project is different, so we offer several motorisation options to match your lifestyle and level of automation. From a straightforward remote through to full smart home integration, we can design a system that feels natural and easy to use.

  • Remote Control: Control one or multiple blinds with a simple handheld remote.
  • Smart Home Hub: Link blinds to a central hub for app and scene control.
  • Smart Home Operating Solutions: Use timers, routines and automation rules.
  • Combine different motorisation options within the same home
  • Upgrade existing blinds to smart operation where possible
  • Ideal for living rooms, bedrooms, offices and commercial spaces
