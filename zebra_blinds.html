Duplex (Zebra) Blinds for Day & Night Control

Zebra blinds are made from a double layer of fabric with alternating sheer and solid stripes. By aligning or offsetting these stripes, you can easily switch between soft filtered light and full privacy without lifting the blind all the way up. If you want to dive deeper into how they work, where to use them and what to choose, you can read our in-depth Zebra Blinds Buying Guide.

During the day, the sheer sections let daylight into the room while still softening glare. At night, the solid bands overlap to create a more private, darker environment. With a wide choice of colours, patterns and textures, zebra blinds add a modern, tidy look to living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens and offices.

Why Choose Zebra Blinds from CaptainBlinds?

Our zebra blinds are manufactured using high-quality polyester and metallic yarns and are fully customized to the exact dimensions of your windows. We measure your windows, help you choose the right fabric and colour, and then have your blinds made-to-order and professionally installed for a perfect fit.

  • Flexible day/night light control in one blind
  • Helps block up to 99.9% of UV rays when fully closed
  • Modern, space-saving design with clean lines
  • Optional motorized operation and smart home control
