Wood Blinds for Warmth & Character

Wood blinds bring a natural warmth and timeless style to your windows. With horizontal slats made from real wood or high-quality faux wood, they offer a classic look that works beautifully in living rooms, dining rooms, bedrooms and home offices.

By tilting the slats, you can easily control how much light enters the room, reduce glare and maintain privacy while still enjoying your outside view. When fully raised, wood blinds stack neatly at the top of the window to open up the full glass area.

Why Choose Wood Blinds from CaptainBlinds?

Our wood blinds are custom-made to fit your windows and are available in a range of slat sizes, stains and painted finishes. You can choose from rich wood tones that highlight the grain or clean painted colours that coordinate with trim and furniture.

We offer both real wood and faux wood options, so you can enjoy the look you love in every room – including areas with higher humidity, such as kitchens or bathrooms, where a moisture-resistant faux wood material is a better choice.

  • Warm, natural look that suits classic and modern interiors
  • Precise control of light and privacy with tilting slats
  • Choice of real wood or durable faux wood materials
  • Multiple slat sizes, stains and paint colours

Product Features & Installation

  • Custom-made to your exact window measurements
  • Available in different slat widths to match scale and style
  • Real wood for living spaces, faux wood for moisture-prone areas
  • Optional decorative tapes for a more tailored, soft look
  • Smooth lifting and tilting systems for everyday use
  • Measured and installed by our professional team in Toronto & the GTA

How to Order Wood Blinds

Ordering wood blinds with CaptainBlinds is simple. Contact us by phone or through our quote form and tell us which rooms you’re working on. We’ll arrange a free in-home consultation to show you finish samples, discuss slat sizes and lift options, and take precise measurements of your windows.

  • Choose real wood or faux wood materials
  • Select slat width, colour and finish
  • Decide on corded, cord-free or motorized operation
  • Custom-made for each individual window
  • Professionally installed for a level, secure fit
  • Designed for long-lasting comfort and everyday use

Delivery & Fitting Guide

After your selections are confirmed, your wood blinds are produced to your exact sizes and prepared for installation. When they’re ready, our installers return to fit the blinds, check the lift and tilt operation, and make final adjustments so the slats close evenly and the headrail sits straight.

For details on production times, measuring and installation, or to schedule your free consultation, call us at (416)985-3839 or send us a message through our contact form.
