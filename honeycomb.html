Honeycomb Shades for Comfort & Efficiency

Honeycomb (cellular) shades trap air inside their cells to help insulate your windows, reduce drafts, and soften outside noise. They bring a clean, modern look to living rooms, bedrooms, nurseries, and home offices while giving you precise light control.

Choose light-filtering fabrics for a soft glow or blackout fabrics for deeper sleep. Single- and double-cell constructions let you balance insulation with a slimmer stack when raised. Cordless and motorized options keep the look tidy and safer for kids and pets.

Why Choose Honeycomb Shades from CaptainBlinds?

Every shade is custom-made to your exact window measurements and installed by our team for a level, secure fit. We bring fabric and opacity samples to your home so you can see how each option handles light, privacy, and colour in your space.

Our experts guide you on lift styles, top-down/bottom-up configurations, and motorization so you can dial in the right balance of privacy, daylight, and convenience for every room.

  • Energy-efficient design that helps reduce drafts and heat gain
  • Light-filtering to blackout options for every room need
  • Cordless and motorized lifts for a clean, child-safe finish
  • Custom colours and cell sizes to match your decor

Product Features & Installation

  • Custom-made to your exact measurements
  • Single or double cells for insulation and slimmer stacks
  • Light-filtering, room-darkening, and blackout fabrics
  • Top-down/bottom-up, cordless, and motorized lift options
  • Measured and installed by our professional team in Toronto & the GTA

How to Order Honeycomb Shades

Start with a quick call or message to tell us about your rooms and goals. We’ll schedule a free in-home consultation, bring fabric and opacity samples, show you cordless and motorized options, and take precise measurements for every window.

  • Pick light-filtering or blackout fabrics
  • Choose single or double-cell construction
  • Select cordless, top-down/bottom-up, or motorized lifts
  • Custom-sized for every window in your home
  • Professionally installed for smooth, level operation
  • Perfect for energy savings, privacy, and quiet comfort

Delivery & Fitting Guide

Once you’ve approved your selections, your honeycomb shades are custom-made to size. Our installers return to fit each shade, verify smooth lift operation, and ensure the headrails sit level and secure.
