Welcome to CaptainBlinds

Custom-Fitted Window Furnishings for Your Home

At CaptainBlinds, we focus on understanding how you live in your space and then matching you with the right window coverings at competitive prices. We bring together locally made products with carefully selected fabrics and systems from leading manufacturers in the UK, Germany, Turkey and South Korea.

Our range includes innovative, stylish and energy-efficient blinds, shades, shutters, aluminium and wood blinds. Whether you are a homeowner, contractor or hospitality client, we offer both custom and ready-made solutions backed by a team that genuinely prioritizes customer satisfaction.

  • Diverse blinds, shades & shutters
  • Custom-fitted to every window
  • Options for homes & contractors
  • Competitive pricing without compromise

Built Around Your Needs & Budget

Our collection has grown to include blinds, shades, shutters, aluminium and wood blinds, with both custom and ready-made options. This allows us to serve simple projects and complex ones with the same level of quality.

  • Innovative, stylish and energy-efficient designs

Serving Homeowners & Professional Clients
