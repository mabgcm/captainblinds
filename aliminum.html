Aluminum Blinds for Modern, Practical Light Control

Aluminum blinds – often known as aluminum Venetian or mini blinds – are made from slim, lightweight metal slats that can be tilted, raised or lowered to control light and privacy. The sleek, compact design works beautifully in both residential and commercial interiors, from kitchens and bathrooms to offices, apartments and rental properties.

Because the slats are made from moisture-resistant, rust-resistant aluminum, these blinds perform especially well in areas with humidity or frequent temperature changes. They are easy to clean, long-lasting and a smart choice when you want a modern, minimal look with precise light management.

Why Choose Aluminum Blinds from CaptainBlinds?

Our aluminum blinds are custom-made to your window sizes and installed by professionals for a clean, level finish. We offer a range of slat widths, colours and finishes so you can match your blinds to your décor and choose the amount of light control you prefer. Whether you need durable blinds for an office, moisture-resistant blinds for a kitchen or bathroom, or a cost-effective solution for multiple rooms, aluminum blinds are a practical, budget-friendly option.

  • Lightweight yet durable metal slats that resist warping and cracking
  • Excellent light and privacy control with tilting slats
  • Moisture-resistant and easy to wipe clean
  • Ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, offices and rental properties
  • Available in a variety of colours and finishes to suit your style

Product Features & Installation

  • Horizontal aluminum slats for precise control of light and glare
  • Durable finish designed to resist fading, corrosion and everyday wear
  • Compact stack when raised, preserving maximum window view
  • Operated with cord or wand for tilting, raising and lowering
  • Suitable for inside or outside mount depending on window depth
  • Measured and installed by our professional team in Toronto & the GTA

How to Order Aluminum Blinds

Ordering aluminum blinds with CaptainBlinds is straightforward. Get in touch by phone, message or through our quote form and tell us about your rooms and windows. We’ll arrange a free in-home consultation to show you slat samples, colours and finish options, and to take accurate measurements before your blinds are manufactured.

  • Choose slat width, colour and finish
  • Match blinds to your interior style and existing hardware
  • Decide on inside or outside mount for each window
  • Custom-made for each individual window opening
  • Professional installation for smooth operation and a tidy look
  • Designed for long-lasting performance with minimal maintenance

Delivery & Fitting Guide

After you confirm your selections, your aluminum blinds are produced to size and prepared for installation. Our installers then return to fit the blinds, adjust the headrail and slats, and make sure operation is smooth and level. We’ll show you how to tilt, raise and lower the blinds and share simple cleaning tips to keep them looking their best.
