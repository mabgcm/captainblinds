Custom Curtains & Drapes & Drapery

Custom curtains add softness, warmth, and tailored style to any room. We create full-length drapery, sheers, and blackout panels to your exact measurements with fabrics that fit your décor.

Choose light-filtering sheers for an airy daytime glow, lined drapery for added privacy, or blackout options for bedrooms and media rooms. Our team measures, fabricates, and installs across Toronto & the GTA.

Light Control, Privacy & Comfort

Layering sheers with drapery lets you shape light throughout the day. Close panels for privacy and glare reduction, or keep sheers drawn to soften sunlight without blocking the view.

  • Sheer, light-filtering, and blackout fabric options
  • Optional privacy, thermal, and blackout linings
  • Single or double-rod setups for layered looks
  • Custom lengths, widths, and heading styles

Key Features & Options

  • Made-to-measure panels for every window size
  • Wide fabric library with textures, patterns, and solids
  • Pleat styles including pinch, ripplefold, grommet, and rod pocket
  • Hardware choices: decorative rods or smooth tracks
  • Optional tiebacks, trims, and layered sheers
  • Professional measuring, fabrication, and installation

How to Choose Your Curtains & Drapes

We’ll help you select fabrics, linings, and hardware that match your room and lifestyle. During a free in-home consultation, we review light needs, privacy goals, and décor so your curtains look beautiful and work perfectly.

  • Select fabric weight and opacity for each room
  • Choose lining options for privacy, blackout, or thermal control
  • Pick a heading style to match your design
  • Decide on rods or tracks and hardware finishes
  • Add tiebacks or trims for a finished look
  • Measured and installed by our experienced team

Delivery & Fitting Guide

Once your fabrics and options are approved, your curtains are made to your exact measurements. Our installers return to hang the panels, adjust the hardware, and ensure the drape looks even and smooth.

For more information about lead times, measuring and installation, or to schedule a free consultation in Toronto & the GTA, call us at (416)985-3839 or contact us through our online form.
