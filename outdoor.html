Outdoor Shades for Patios, Decks & Pergolas

Outdoor Shades are one of our most popular products for transforming outdoor spaces into cozy, comfortable areas. With the right exterior window treatment, your patio, deck or pergola can become the perfect spot to relax, entertain or enjoy a quiet moment at any time of day.

These exterior shades are made with mesh and sheer fabrics that block UV rays, reduce glare and filter light while still preserving your view. By reducing heat and harsh sunlight, they help keep porches, balconies and decks cooler and more enjoyable, while also protecting your outdoor furniture and décor from fading and sun damage.

Why Choose Outdoor Shades from CaptainBlinds?

Our Outdoor Shades are fully customized to the dimensions of your openings, whether they are standard windows, wide patio doors or open-sided structures. We measure your space, help you select the fabric, colour and openness level, and then have your shades manufactured to size and installed by our experienced team.

  • Mesh and sheer fabrics that block UV and reduce glare
  • Helps minimize heat build-up in outdoor living areas
  • Preserves outward views while improving comfort
  • Protects outdoor furniture and décor from sun damage
  • Custom-made to fit patios, decks, pergolas and balconies

Comfortable Outdoor Living All Day Long

Window treatments aren’t just for the inside of your home. Exterior solar shades are a practical, stylish addition to patios and decks, giving you more hours of use by softening the light and improving privacy. With Outdoor Shades from CaptainBlinds, you can enjoy fresh air and outdoor views without sacrificing comfort.

Product Features & Installation

  • Durable, weather-resistant materials designed for exterior use
  • Mesh and sheer options to balance shade, privacy and view
  • Suitable for patios, decks, pergolas, balconies and large openings
  • Smooth operation with reliable hardware and components
  • Easy to clean and maintain for long-term performance
  • Measured, manufactured and installed by professionals in Toronto & the GTA

How to Order Outdoor Shades

Ordering Outdoor Shades with CaptainBlinds is a simple, guided process. Get in touch with us by phone, message or through our quote form and tell us about your outdoor space and how you use it. We’ll schedule a free consultation to show you fabric options, explain light and heat control, and take precise measurements before your shades are produced.

  • Choose fabric type, colour and openness level
  • Match your shades to your exterior style and structure
  • Decide on the best mounting and operation for your space
  • Have each shade custom-made for a perfect fit
  • Installation handled by experienced professionals
  • Enjoy cooler, more comfortable outdoor living areas

Delivery & Fitting Guide

After your fabric and configuration choices are confirmed, your Outdoor Shades are manufactured to your exact sizes and prepared for installation. Our team then returns to fit the shades, test operation and make any final adjustments so they run smoothly and look neat and level.

For details on production times, measuring and installation, or to book your free consultation, call us at (437) 995-5552 or contact us through our online form.
