Graphic Blinds & Custom Printed Roller Shades

Graphic blinds (also called custom printed or photo roller shades) let you turn any window into a design feature or branding surface. Instead of a plain fabric, the shade becomes a full-colour canvas where we can print logos, photos, artwork or patterns directly onto the blind fabric using digital printing technology.

These blinds work just like standard roller shades, smoothly rolling up and down for everyday light control. The difference is the printed design: you can display your company logo and contact details in a shopfront, showcase a large image in a reception area, or add a favourite photograph or artwork to a living room or media room. As a result, graphic blinds are popular for both homes and commercial spaces that want to stand out.

Where Graphic Blinds Work Best

Graphic blinds are ideal for street-facing windows, boardrooms, clinics, cafés, salons, fitness studios and retail stores where you want to combine shade, privacy and visual impact. In residential spaces they work beautifully in kids’ rooms, home offices, game rooms and home theatres, where a bold print or image can set the tone for the entire space.

How to Order Graphic Blinds

Ordering graphic blinds with CaptainBlinds starts with a conversation about your windows and your artwork. You can supply your own high-resolution files, or we can help you evaluate whether an image is suitable for printing at your required size.   • Confirm fabric type (screen, light-filtering or blackout)

Once your design, fabric and sizes are approved, your graphic blinds are printed and assembled as custom roller shades.
